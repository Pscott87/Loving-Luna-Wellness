Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle Japanese healing technique focused on channeling universal energy to support relaxation, clarity, emotional release, and overall well-being.

Do I need to prepare anything for my session?

Simply wear comfortable clothing and arrive with an open mind. If it's a distance session, choose a quiet space where you can relax without interruptions.

How long are sessions?

Typical sessions range from 45–75 minutes depending on the service. You can choose what feels right for your needs.

Is Reiki safe?

Yes! Reiki is non-invasive and safe for all ages. It complements medical care beautifully but is not a replacement for professional treatment.
