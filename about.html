About Loving Luna Wellness

Loving Luna Wellness was created as a sanctuary for anyone seeking emotional balance, spiritual support, and gentle energetic healing. Rooted in the calming symbolism of the moon, this practice offers a soft, nurturing approach to well-being — inviting you back into alignment with your own natural rhythm.

Through the practice of distance Reiki, your energy is supported no matter where you are physically. Sessions are guided intuitively and with compassion, helping you release tension, restore clarity, and reconnect to your inner peace.

This is a space where you can breathe, reset, and feel held.
